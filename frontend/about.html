📘 About QCryptLab

Your gateway to understanding Quantum Computing and Cryptography through interactive, authentic simulations.

🚀 Our Mission

QCryptLab empowers learners with hands-on experiences that demystify quantum computing and cryptography through rich simulations and intuitive learning tools.

🧭 Learning Path

  • Quantum Computing Foundations – Explore qubits, superposition, entanglement, and measurement.
  • Visual Qubits – Use Bloch sphere visualizations and interactive state simulations.
  • Quantum Gates – Understand single- and multi-qubit gates with live interaction.
  • Quantum Circuit Builder – Drag, drop, and simulate your own quantum circuits.
  • BB84 Protocol Simulation – Experience quantum key distribution live with Alice, Bob, and Eve.
  • Security & Analysis – Dive into quantum security techniques and error detection.

🛠️ Platform Features

  • Interactive, real-time tutorials with guided feedback.
  • Authentic Python backend powered by Qiskit & QuTiP.
  • Advanced visualizations including Bloch spheres and circuit diagrams.
  • Error handling with educational messages for better understanding.
  • Export options for analysis (JSON, CSV, PDF).
  • Mobile-friendly and accessible design.

🔧 Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python Flask with Qiskit & QuTiP
  • Visualization: Matplotlib served via API

📘 Getting the Most Out of QCryptLab

  • Follow the learning path from beginner to advanced modules.
  • Interact with simulations to deepen your understanding.
  • Experiment freely to observe quantum principles in action.
  • Report issues or suggest features on GitHub.
